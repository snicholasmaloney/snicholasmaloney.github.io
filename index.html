BIO

S Nicholas Maloney


S Nicholas Maloney is a Media Specialist in the United States Army. After Basic Training, Nicholas completed a variety of advanced media courses for the Defense Information School (DINFOS) at Fort George G. Meade. As an E-4 media engineer in the US Army, Nicholas is a confident videographer and still photographer; he has documented numerous missions, various military operations and commemorative ceremonies for the United States government. After five years he had the opportunity to return to DINFOS for advanced storytelling and video production training. Currently enrolled in Audio Video Media Production classes at Howard County Community college to complete his degree, Nicholas plans to attend Military Motion Media program at Syracuse University next year.
